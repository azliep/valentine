TO MY VALENTINE

MUAH! 

                    A Poem?!


                    this poem is about how i love ngawang tobden
                    although i don't or rarely show it back then
                    he made me feel special, and for me he's the best
                    he's the sweetest, charming, and just the kindest
                    boy that I ever met

                    they say the sun is a star too
                    and he is my sun in the night
                    that i cry myself to sleep
                    with a light that's just right
                    to keep me from the deep

                    he's always there and made me feel less alone
                    through him that i'm more open and able to see
                    what i couldnt before through just our phones
                    wishing he could see what he really means to me

                    wishing he'd stop pushing me away
                    rest assured that i will stay
                    and gravitate him towards me
                    as we spin around this galaxy

                    he tells me he's okay even though I know he's not
                    but i guess i couldn't have a say in that as well
                    got caught up in my own world amidst all the dust
                    so used to hiding away from the unfamiliar hell

                    the future to those who wallow in the dark
                    isn't as bright as the fire that ignites him
                    hoping the fire doesnt consume him from within
                    though i know that we all are uncertain to win

                    he taught me unconditional love
                    i once found cheezy and stupid
                    look at us now, how funny it flipped
                    like the stars that collided and just like that
                    i found my soulmate that would be a tragic to bid
